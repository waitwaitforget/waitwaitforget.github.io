Biography

I am Kai Tian (Chinese: 田凯), a fifth-year PhD student at Fudan Univeristy, my supervisor is Prof. Shuigeng Zhou. Prior to that, I got my B.S. degree from East China University of Science and Technology. My research interests include unsupervised learning, semi-supervised deep learning. I am also interested in exploring the cooperation and competition among neural network models.
